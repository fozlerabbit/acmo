Mission & Approach

We combine rights‑based advocacy, safeguarding, and employability skills to keep girls in education and empower boys as allies.

Awareness

Campaigns in schools, madrasas, and communities with context‑aware content.

Safeguarding

Referral pathways and rapid response with local authorities.

Skills

Digital, leadership, and life‑skills training aligned to SDGs.

Evidence

Monitoring dashboards and youth‑led research for policy wins.

Flagship Programmes

School & Community Workshops

Interactive sessions for students, parents, and community leaders covering law, health, and alternatives to early marriage.

  • Age‑appropriate curriculum (11–18)
  • Male engagement & bystander training
  • Safeguarding signposting

Youth Advocate Network

Train student leaders to run micro‑campaigns, gather evidence, and liaise with local government.

Helpline & Referral

Confidential support with escalation protocols and partner NGOs.

Skills for Her

Digital skills, entrepreneurship, and financial literacy to expand choices.
